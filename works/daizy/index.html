Daizy

Comissioned (sort of…)

Having found a way to create a fairly realistic daisy shape, I hit that same realisation I often do of “I have not really planned beyond this point, and have no idea what to do next to turn this into something vaguely appealing”.

Maybe it’s just me, but when I feel like I have come up with an interesting, nuanced idea, it often turns out that my brain has just “filled in the gaps” and actually the idea needs much more thought. I imagine this is a bit like how the brain handles dreams - glossing over the bits that are missing or don’t make sense so we don’t question them at the time. I hope coming up with ideas gets easier with practice, but from what I have read this is not necessarily the case!
