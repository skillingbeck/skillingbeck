Daizy

Comissioned (sort of…)

Having found a way to create a fairly realistic daisy shape, I hit that same realisation I often do, which is something along the lines of “I have not really planned beyond this point, and have no idea what to do next”.

Maybe it’s just me, but I regularly find that the idea as I imagined it is nowhere near as detailed or refined as I thought it was when it comes to the reality of implementing it. It seems to be one of those tricks of the mind.
